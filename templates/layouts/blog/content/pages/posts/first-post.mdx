# The Speed of Static Sites

When it comes to delivering content, speed is everything. Slow pages lead to high bounce rates and lower conversions.

## Why Static is Faster
- **No Database Overhead**: Traditional sites query a database for every page request. Static sites are compiled once and served directly as raw HTML/CSS.
- **Global CDN Delivery**: Static files can be cached and distributed globally to the edge, close to your users.
- **Improved Security**: Without an active database or server-side scripting runtime, the attack surface is virtually zero.
